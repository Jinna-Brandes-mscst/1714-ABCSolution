User Stories


As a DSP, I want to enter participants timecards so there is a record of their time.

As a DSP, I want to have access to all job numbers(ID's) from a search result.

As a PC, I want to look at participants timecards for the whole month.

As a DSP, I want to enter participants outcomes at the same time as their timecards.

As a PC, I want to make sure DSP's are entering outcome information on a weekly basis.
